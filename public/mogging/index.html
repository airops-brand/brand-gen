Mar 1 – 31, 2026 Slack — all channels + DMs Compiled Mar 31

Somewhere between Gen Z internet culture and a company Slack that has no guardrails, a vocabulary took root. Maxxing is the act of optimizing a single trait to its absolute limit — as if the human body were a Notion doc with one field left to fill in. Mogging is what happens when someone does it so well that everyone else in the room becomes a background character. Together, they form a worldview: there is a hierarchy, there is a metric, and Will is winning. This document is a forensic record of how that worldview quietly colonized AirOps Slack over the course of one month — one message, one emoji, one NYT clavicle article at a time.
